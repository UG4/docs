libDiscretization

About

libDiscretization is a library to provide Disretization tools that work on grids and algebras provided by libGrid and libAlgebra and is the third part of ug. libDiscretization depends on both libraries, while the other two are independent of each other and of libDiscretization.

Currently available topics:

  • Assembling Concept
  • Discrete Function Handling
  • Integration Point Data
