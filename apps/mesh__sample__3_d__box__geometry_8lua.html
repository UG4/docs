Script to create a sample 3D box geometry using plugin ProMesh consisting of (1) a thin box on top of the larger box, (2) a tall box inside the large box that sits on the bottom of the large box, (3) a box inside a box sitting on the bottom of the large box, and (4) a sphere inside a sphere sitting on the bottom of the large box

To execute type: ugshell -ex mesh_sample_3D_box_geometry.lua
